Instructions

Objective

This is one v one player game in one keyboared ,players goal is to reach the white area of the oppnent's side or just kill with no mercy and win the game

Controls

For the player on right : shoot wth Enter, move with arrow keys. For the left side player : shoot with spacebar, move with WASD.

Game Over

You lose the game if your health reaches zero or if the player reaches one one of the opponents area.
